About us

Entrepreneurship Cell nits is set up to foster entrepreneurship among the students. It is like a club, run by students and driven by the management of the institutes.Students learn important & useful trait about entrepreneurship. Even though students do not take up entrepreneurship as a full-time profession, the skill sets learned as E-Cell members would be extremely helpful to them in any job.

Events

E-Cells conduct several activities such as workshops, interaction with successful entrepreneurs, building prototypes, setting up small campus companies where other students can be your customers, internship opportunities with outside companies etc.

STORK MARKET WORKSHOP

“We are very much familiar with the term, but the details of Stock Market are a mystery. Through this workshop one will be learn the basics and would be able start exploring this seemingly much attractive domain. A stock market, equity market or share market is the aggregation of buyers and sellers (a loose network of economic transactions, not a physical entity or discrete entity) of stocks (also called shares). which represents ownership claims on businesses; these may include securities listed on a public stock exchanged a wall as stock that is only traded privately. If you want to be a Stock Market mogul and learn the trickery behind stock management, this workshop will be a steppingstone for all future Wall Street tycoons. Not only the knowledge, but also a real-life simulation of a big market will be used to test your craft. You can apply for the workshop as a team of three people with knowledge to be taken in and big prizes to be won!

BUSINESS MODEL CANVAS

With the very same perspective, SRiJaN 1.0 is all set to organise an event 'Business model canvas competition' which will facilitate the student entrepreneurs to learn about the strategic management of business and showcase their potentials in this arena. Business model canvas simplify the complex business model structures. This competition is to be conducted groupwise. The strength of each group must be 3-4. In this competition, groups need to represent their ideas of business models using a single sheet of paper and finally the one with most efficient canvas will be selected for top prize. So, roll your sleeves up to witness such a magnificent event.

BOARD ROOM
